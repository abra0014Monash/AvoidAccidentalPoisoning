Do you know that people are more likely to die due to accidental poisoning than road traffic in Australia?

Which drugs are the top culprits? Opioids followed by benzodiazepines.

Which opioids kill the most? Oxycodone/Morphine/Codeine

Accidental Poisoning killed twice as many men as women

Which drug-mix kills the most? Opioids + benzodiazepines
