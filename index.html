Are you at Risk?


What is accidental poisoning?

WHY IT MATTERS?

  • Accidental poisoning is about people unintentionally poisoning themselves including accidental drug overdose.
  • Premature death is defined as death that occurs before 75.
  • Accidental poisoning is the second leading cause of premature death among adults aged 25-44.
  • There were 1,612 unintentional drug-induced deaths in 2017, more than double the death toll of 725 by car crashes in Australia.

What causes accidental overdose?

Interactions between alcohol-drug, disease-drug, drug-drug
Alcohol Interaction
Alcohol consumption in conjunction with drug use could contribute to fatal overdose
Disease Interaction
Some drugs could have adverse effects on pre-existing disease condition
Drug Interaction
Drug to drug interaction could lead to fatal overdose
